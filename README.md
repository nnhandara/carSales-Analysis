# CarSales-Dashboard

**Project Overview**
The Car Sales Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence project developed to analyse car sales performance, customer behaviour, and company revenue contribution.
The dashboard provides insights into:
•	Sales trends over time
•	Customer segmentation and preferences
•	Regional performance
•	Brand-level revenue analysis
•	Transmission and colour preference patterns
The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support strategic business decision-making.

**Project Objectives**
•	Analyse overall sales performance and revenue trends
•	Identify seasonal sales patterns
•	Understand customer demographics and preferences
•	Evaluate company/brand-level revenue contribution
•	Provide data-driven recommendations for business growth

**Dashboard Sections**

**1) Sales Overview**
This section provides a high-level summary of performance using key KPIs:
•	Total Customers
•	Total Cars Sold
•	Average Car Price
•	Total Annual Income
Additional visualizations include:
•	Monthly sales trends
•	Annual income by company
•	Revenue by region
•	Revenue comparison by transmission type
**Key Insight:**
Sales peak in Q4 (November–December), and Automatic vehicles generate higher revenue compared to Manual vehicles.

**2) Customer Analysis**
This section explores customer demographics and buying behavior:
•	Customer distribution by gender
•	Gear type preference by gender
•	Colour preference analysis
•	Customer distribution by region
**Key Insight:**
The customer base is predominantly male, and neutral car colors (Pale White & Black) are most preferred.

**3️) Company Analysis**
This section focuses on brand and revenue performance:
•	Total annual income by company
•	Monthly revenue trends
•	Detailed transactional breakdown
**Key Insight:**
Revenue is concentrated among a few top-performing brands, indicating both strength and potential dependency risk.

**Key Findings**
•	Strong overall annual income performance
•	Significant seasonal sales spike in Q4
•	Automatic transmission vehicles dominate revenue
•	Male customers represent the majority segment
•	Neutral colours drive higher demand
•	Revenue diversification across regions reduces risk

**Business Recommendations**
•	Introduce promotional campaigns during low-performing months (Q1)
•	Expand marketing strategies targeting female customers
•	Increase inventory of Automatic transmission vehicles
•	Align stock planning with colour demand trends
•	Strengthen underperforming brands through repositioning
•	Implement predictive analytics for demand forecasting
